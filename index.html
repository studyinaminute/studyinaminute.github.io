About Us

Huh? Another site for NCERT answers?

As a matter of fact, no. Since the very start, we believed in creating a site for learning rather than teaching. A site where students can learn a topic instead of copying the answers.

We still had to make our site different from thousands of lectures available on the internet. We were able to solve this by drastically decreasing the time taken to learn a topic. This was achieved through appointing the most talented teachers to explain the chapters.
